ABOUT NITPY

The coast of Bay of Bengal witnessed the rise of a new era of technical education as one of India's Institute of national importance, National Institute of Technology, Puducherry, got established in 2010, at Karaikal. Karaikal, formerly known for its diverse culture and cuisines is now redefining the standards of technical education.

ABOUT GYANITH

Gyanith, to us, is much more than our technical festival. It is the manifestation of our collective dreams, ambitions, ideas and passion, all of it crammed into a power packed four days of workshops, technical showcases, hackathons, quizzes and various other technical competitions. Not only that, Gyanith also packs cultural presentations and various pro shows. The fest is as diverse as us NITians, bustling with ideas from every corner of the nation. With every passing year, Gyanith grows in scale, duration, diversity and ambition, alluring visitors from afar who wish to witness our techno spectacle. This year the ambitious spirit towers more than ever, with a greater number and rich diversity of workshops and events from all the departments, covering every cutting edge niche of the tech industry. Wherever your interests lie, we sure got you covered. Come join us for our energetic and informative techno carnival.

HISTORY OF GYANITH

NIT Puducherry is famous for its advancements made in technical fields and a major contribution is done through the Institute's proudest event, Gyanith. Since it's inception in 2017, Gyanith is setting up a new benchmark every year, in the number of events as well as in the number of participants. Spreading its fame across Puducherry, Tamil Nadu, and Kerala, Gyanith is now ready to be India's largest sought technical platform, with its.. 4th edition, where it comes up with more events, newer and better! After the curtain raiser of Gyanith '20, the highlights are many workshops on hot and trending topics in technical fields, handled by the person of expertise from the respective areas. Apart from the technical events, Gyanith is a platform to bring up young and talented brains from the school benches through its science expos and project expos areawaiting the students pursuing undergraduate and higher degrees. Also with a big fat stage with non restricting mike, we welcome all the proficient performers to come and entertain our great audience through our non technical events.Gyanith '20 is this undoubtedly, a great opportunity to showcase and widen your vistas of knowledge.
